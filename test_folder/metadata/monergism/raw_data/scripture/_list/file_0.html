This month we celebrate our 25th Anniversary - We Thank the Lord for You.

By God’s grace, Monergism has become the foremost global hub for Reformed literature.Over the past 25 years, it has served as the go-to destination for pastors, scholars, and lay Christians seeking sound, Christ-centered theology—all in one accessible, online space. We provide unparalleled access to timeless teachings and resources free of charge so that cost is never a barrier to building a rich Christian library.".

Last year alone, over 1 million new visitors accessed Monergism’s resources. From pastors preparing sermons in remote villages to new believers growing in faith, this ministry serves countless people worldwide who could never afford such a library on their own.

Yet sustaining this ministry requires your support. Given that less than 1% of readers donate, we humbly ask you to consider joining those who keep this work alive. If everyone reading this gave just $5, we would meet our 2024 budget in no time.

Every contribution makes an eternal impact—whether it’s a monthly gift of $20 or a one-time donation of $10, $25, $100, or more. Your generosity ensures that Monergism can continue to equip the global church with Christ-centered, gospel-rich resources for generations to come. You can read our business plan to see how your gifts are used to further this mission and support our vision for vastly improving Monergism’s website and resources in 2025.

Monergism (CPRF) is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, and all donations are tax-deductible.
